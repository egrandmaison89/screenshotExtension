Privacy Policy

SnapPage — Full Page Screenshot · Last updated: February 2026

This privacy policy applies to the SnapPage Full Page Screenshot Chrome extension.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account registration required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No external servers or third-party services
  • No data is sent off your device

What the Extension Does

The extension captures screenshots of web pages when you explicitly click the extension icon and choose to capture. All processing happens locally on your computer. Screenshots are saved only to your Downloads/Screenshots folder.

Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — Access the current tab only when you click the extension icon to capture a screenshot
  • downloads — Save screenshot files to your Downloads folder
  • scripting — Inject a content script to coordinate scrolling during capture

The extension does not access any tabs, pages, or data unless you initiate a capture.

Open Source

The extension source code is publicly available on GitHub. You can review exactly what the extension does.
